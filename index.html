Dedicated to providing love, care, and opportunities for children in need since 2012.

About the founders

Mr. Nicholas Osamokuwa Woghiren, along with Isoken Joy Onaiwu Woghiren established this orphanage as a fulfillment of his late motherâ€™s desire and dream. Late Madam Grace Woghiren Obamedo was a visionary and philanthropist. She gave freely and willingly to all those who were in need. Her main focus was caring for children and the elderly. She cared for all her children, teaching them the value of hard work as well as caring for those in need. She was an astute and successful businesswoman and had over 100 employees working for her. She provided scholarships to deserving children of her employees and a lot of them today are successful members of society. To ensure her legacy lives on and to make sure her dream of giving every child a path to reach excellence, an orphanage has been born in her name.

Mission Statement

At Madam Grace Woghiren Obamedo Orphanage Memorial Home we honor the legacy and vision of our founder's mother by providing a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for vulnerable children. Our mission is to give hope to these young lives by empowering them with education, healthcare, and unconditional love, helping them grow into confident, capable, and compassionate members of society who can reach their full potential!

Our Programs

Our Story

Madam Grace Woghiren Obamedo Memorial Orphanage Home was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide every child with the love, care, and opportunities they deserve. We believe that every child has the potential to achieve greatness when given the right support and environment.

Over the years, we've helped hundreds of children find their path in life through education, emotional support, and a loving community that believes in their potential.
